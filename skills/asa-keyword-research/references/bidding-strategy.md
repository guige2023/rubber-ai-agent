# ASA Bidding & CPA Strategy

Expert-level ASA delivery is not just about the quality of keywords, but the art of bidding.

## 1. Core Bidding Logic

### CPT (Cost Per Tap) - Base Bid
This is the maximum price you are willing to pay for each click.

- **Brand**: **High Bid Strategy**. Recommended to set at 1.5x - 2x of the system's recommended value, or directly set a high protective price (e.g., $2.00+) to ensure 100% Share of Voice (SOV).
- **Generic**: **Moderate/Balanced Strategy**. Start based on the average level of the app category (e.g., $0.50 - $1.50) and adjust backward based on the conversion rate.
- **Competitor**: **Aggressive Strategy**. Usually 20-30% higher than generic keywords to attempt conquesting in competitor territory.

### CPA Goal (Cost Per Acquisition) & ROI Calculation
This is the average cost you expect to spend per download. Never guess a CPA; calculate it based on the subscription price to ensure profitability.

**Empirical ROI Formula (The Standard):**
Based on extensive historical data, use these baseline metrics if the user doesn't provide their own:
1. **New User Signup Rate** (Conversion to Paid): 10%
2. **Average Renewals**: 4 times
3. **Apple Tax**: 15%

**Calculation Steps:**
1. **Net Revenue per Sub** = `Subscription Price × (1 - 0.15)`
2. **Lifetime Value (LTV)** = `Net Revenue × 4`
3. **Break-even CPA** = `LTV × 10%`
   - *(Simplified Formula: `Break-even CPA = Subscription Price × 0.34`)*
4. **Target CPA (Launch Phase)** = `Break-even CPA × 0.8`
   - *Logic: Reserve a 20% profit margin. During the cold start/launch phase, we accept a higher CPA to test the waters and acquire data.*

- **Cold Start Recommendation**: Start bidding based on this 80% Target CPA. If a keyword consistently exceeds the Break-even CPA over 3 days, lower the bid.

## 3. Agent Estimation Guidelines
1. **Check Benchmarks**: The agent should search for the latest annual "ASA Benchmark Report" via browser to obtain average conversion rates for specific industries.
2. **Dynamic Adjustment**: If a keyword has a very high SP but intense competition, the recommended bid should be increased by 30%.
3. **Calculation Formula**: Estimated CPT ≈ Target CPA × Estimated Conversion Rate (CR).
   - *Example: If Target CPA is $2.00 and industry average CR is 40%, the recommended CPT = 2.00 * 0.4 = $0.80.*
