# SEO Keyword Research — Scoring & Classification Reference

## Search Intent Decision Tree

```
Is the query about a specific brand/site?
  ├─ YES → 🧭 Navigational
  └─ NO → Does it contain action words (buy, download, signup, price)?
           ├─ YES → 🛒 Transactional
           └─ NO → Does it contain comparison words (best, vs, review, top)?
                    ├─ YES → 💰 Commercial Investigation
                    └─ NO → 🔍 Informational
```

## ICE Scoring Framework

Used in the Action Plan to prioritize recommendations objectively.

**ICE = (Impact + Confidence + Ease) / 3** — Score each dimension 1-10.

| Dimension | Low (1-3) | Medium (4-6) | High (7-10) |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| **Impact** | Minor visibility boost | Moderate traffic gain | Major ranking or conversion lift |
| **Confidence** | Speculative, no data | Some SERP signals | Clear gap, validated by competitor |
| **Ease** | Requires dev work or 3000+ words | Research + quality writing | Metadata fix or quick page |

### ICE Examples

| Action | I | C | E | ICE | Verdict |
|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|:--|
| Fix H1 to include primary keyword | 8 | 9 | 10 | **9.0** | 🔥 Do immediately |
| Create `/tools/calculator` page | 8 | 7 | 5 | **6.7** | 📊 Plan for Month 1 |
| Write 5000-word pillar guide | 9 | 6 | 3 | **6.0** | 🏔️ Strategic target |

## SERP Feature Impact on CTR

| Feature | Impact on Organic CTR | Opportunity |
|:--|:--|:--|
| AI Overview | 📉 Significant reduction | Target complex, subjective queries |
| Featured Snippet | 📉 Moderate for #2-10 | Win the snippet = massive visibility |
| People Also Ask | ➡️ Neutral | Target as long-tail keywords |
| Video Pack | 📉 Slight reduction | Create video for dual ranking |
| Knowledge Panel | 📉 Heavy reduction | Avoid these keywords |

## Content Type by Intent

| Intent | Recommended Format | Typical Word Count |
|:--|:--|:--|
| 🔍 Informational | How-to guide, tutorial, FAQ | 1500-3000 |
| 🧭 Navigational | Landing page, docs | 300-800 |
| 💰 Commercial | Comparison, "best X" listicle | 2000-4000 |
| 🛒 Transactional | Product page, pricing page | 500-1500 |
